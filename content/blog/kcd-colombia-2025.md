### Why This Combination is a Big Deal

This isn't just a co-location; it's a convergence. You can't have a successful cloud-native strategy without a robust infrastructure layer.

This event directly addresses the key questions on everyone's mind in 2025:

*   **The VMware Migration:** As companies flee Broadcom's new policies, where do they land? This event will be a hotspot for talks on migrating to KubeVirt and OpenStack.
*   **Powering AI at Scale:** The Cloud-Native world (Kubernetes) is how you *manage* AI workloads, but the OpenInfra world (OpenStack, Ironic) is how you *provide* the on-demand, cost-effective GPU and bare-metal resources.
*   **Digital Sovereignty:** For Colombia's booming FinTech and public sectors, this event brings together the OSPO (strategy) and OpenInfra (technology) for building secure, in-country clouds.
*   **A Pure Community Focus:** The event is 100% community-driven and non-profit, meaning the agenda is set by and for the engineers, architects, and innovators who are actively building this technology.
