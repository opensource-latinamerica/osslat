In the fast-paced world of technology, continuous learning is not just an option; it's a necessity. Whether you're looking to start a new career, upskill in your current role, or simply explore new areas of interest, the wealth of free online resources available today makes it easier than ever to achieve your goals. In this post, we've curated a list of platforms and organizations that offer free, high-quality courses in various technology domains.

### The Linux Foundation

The Linux Foundation is at the forefront of open source, and they extend their expertise through a range of free courses. These courses are perfect for beginners and those looking to get a foundational understanding of key open source technologies. You can explore their offerings on the [Linux Foundation website](https://training.linuxfoundation.org/resources/free-courses/).

Some of the free courses offered include:
- **Blockchain: Understanding its Uses and Implications (LFS170)**
- **Introduction to Open Source Networking Technologies (LFS165)**

### Red Hat

Red Hat, a leading provider of enterprise open source solutions, offers a variety of free training courses and resources. These are great for anyone interested in Linux, cloud computing, and automation.

You can find free courses through:
- **LearnQuest:** A Red Hat Business Partner providing free technical overview courses. Find out more on the [LearnQuest website](https://www.learnquest.com/free-red-hat-courses.aspx).
  - Red Hat OpenStack Technical Overview (CL010)
  - Containers, Kubernetes and Red Hat OpenShift Technical Overview (DO080)
  - Red Hat Enterprise Linux Technical Overview (RH024)
- **edX:** Red Hat offers several courses on edX that can be audited for free. Explore them on the [edX platform](https://www.edx.org/school/red-hat).
  - Fundamentals of Red Hat Enterprise Linux
  - Fundamentals of Containers, Kubernetes and OpenShift

### Tetrate Academy

For those interested in service mesh and related technologies, Tetrate Academy offers free courses on Istio and Envoy. Check them out at [Tetrate Academy](https://academy.tetrate.io/collections).

- **TSB100 - Introduction to TSB**
- **CNCF100 - Istio Fundamentals**
- **CNCF200 - Envoy Fundamentals**

### IBM SkillsBuild

IBM SkillsBuild is a free education program that helps adult learners, high school and university students, and faculty develop new skills and find jobs. They have partnered with organizations like Red Hat to provide learning paths in Linux, Cloud, and DevOps. Learn more at the [IBM SkillsBuild website](https://skillsbuild.org/learners).

### Santander Open Academy

Santander Open Academy offers various free courses and scholarships in technology, languages, and professional skills, often in partnership with leading universities and institutions. Discover their programs on the [Santander Open Academy website](https://www.santanderopenacademy.com/en/index.html).

These platforms represent just a fraction of the free learning opportunities available online. We encourage you to explore these resources and take the first step in your learning journey today. Happy learning!
